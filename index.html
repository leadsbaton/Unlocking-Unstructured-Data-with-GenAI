Unlocking Unstructured Data with GenAI

GenAI is revolutionizing how organizations and their customers access, use, and interact with information. With the rapid growth of unstructured data – such as product manuals and knowledge bases – the right information architecture is essential for uncovering, understanding, and leveraging these assets for effective data-driven decision-making.

Implementing GenAI effectively requires overcoming practical challenges. By establishing the right information architecture, you can address key concerns like:

  • Data Connectivity: Bridge gaps between disparate data sources, from on-premise systems like mainframes to ecosystems like SAP and Cloud platforms.
  • Governance and Safety: Ensure AI implementation aligns with your organization's safety and governance requirements without compromise.
  • Cost Management: Adopt strategies to manage costs across the information lifecycle.

Key Takeaways:

  • Practical approaches to integrate diverse data types and formats for streamlined access and usability.
  • Effective tools and techniques to control hidden costs and simplify processes.
  • Strategies to balance innovation with governance to ensure responsible use of your organization's most valuable asset—data.
