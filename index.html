Data Security and Governance for the AI Era

AI is changing how organizations use data, but it’s also creating new risks along the way. Sensitive data is flowing into model pipelines, training sets, third-party APIs and generative outputs. From data leaks in large language models to regulatory scrutiny around AI decision-making, the stakes are getting higher. Traditional security frameworks weren’t built for this. Likewise, governance models need a rethink.

Join us for a special roundtable webinar on September 11th on how to modernize your data strategy for the AI era. You’ll get clear, actionable strategies for protecting sensitive data, ensuring responsible AI usage, and maintaining compliance in a fast-moving landscape.

You'll learn:

  • How AI changes the data risk landscape
  • Key governance frameworks for AI-powered systems
  • Managing sensitive data in training and inference workflows
  • Compliance, transparency, and auditability in the AI lifecycle
  • Tools and best practices for scalable, enforceable governance
